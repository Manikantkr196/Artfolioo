About the Artist

Passionate artist dedicated to capturing the essence of human emotion through pencil and paper.

Myself Manikant kumar, With over 3 years of experience in portrait art, I specialize in creating realistic pencil sketches that tell stories. Each portrait is carefully crafted to capture not just the physical likeness, but the soul and personality of the subject.

My sketching journey began in 10th grade, where I discovered my passion for art and gradually honed my skills through classical drawing techniques and constant practice. Since then, I've had the privilege of creating portraits for families, couples, and individuals who want to preserve their most precious memories.
